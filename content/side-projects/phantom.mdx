Built a state-of-the-art chat & outreach platform for exclusive content platforms.

## Scale

- 2M+ messages handled across 12+ platforms
- Licensed to one of Germany's biggest exclusive content agencies for a % of revenue

## Competitive Edge

While competitors got banned for similar operations with inferior tooling, Phantom kept the agency's operations in stealth.

The platform is completely platform-agnostic, unlike competitors like Infloww that only work with specific platforms.

## Tech Stack

- Electron for cross-platform desktop app
- Next.js for the web dashboard
- TypeScript throughout
- Custom automation and anti-detection systems
